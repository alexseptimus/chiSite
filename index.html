About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Yvon.
I'm an Amateur Artist, my art follows the trends and love for Oriental Style Art.
Moreover i try to fuse together the characteristics of a Contemporary Minimal Art with an Oriental Style Art in my drawings,
in fact in each of these you can find a message about existence on earth that doesn't show up at the first look.
That's why i pay a particular attention to details on the very few things i draw, giving them strong and deep meanings.
